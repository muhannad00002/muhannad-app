SCAN & GO PLATFORM - PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS

Overview A SaaS platform consisting of: 1. Customer App 2. Merchant App
(Store Management)

Customer App - User registration/login. - Select store by scanning
entrance QR. - Scan product QR codes. - View product details. - Shopping
cart management. - Generate Checkout QR. - Cashier scans Checkout QR. -
Payment completion. - Purchase history. - Digital receipts. - Loyalty
support (future).

Merchant App

Dashboard - Sales overview. - Orders. - Revenue. - Top products. -
Active cashiers.

Product Management - Create/Edit/Delete products. - Images. -
Categories. - SKU. - Barcode. - QR generation. - Inventory quantity. -
Tax. - Pricing. - Bulk import/export.

QR Management - Generate printable QR labels. - PDF export. - Batch
printing.

Checkout - Scan customer Checkout QR. - Review cart. - Apply
discounts. - Accept cash/card/payment gateway. - Complete order. -
Print/send receipt.

Inventory - Stock adjustments. - Low stock alerts. - Inventory history.

Reports - Daily/weekly/monthly/yearly sales. - Product performance. -
Employee performance. - Inventory reports.

Employees - Roles and permissions. - Activity logs.

Branches - Multi-branch support.

Settings - Taxes. - Branding. - Receipt template. - Business
information.

MULTI-BRAND BOUTIQUE FEATURE

Many boutiques sell products belonging to different independent
brands/vendors.

Vendor Management - Create vendor accounts. - Assign products to
vendors. - Vendor contact information. - Commission settings (optional).

Automatic Vendor Notifications The system automatically sends a daily
notification/email/WhatsApp summary to every vendor including: -
Products sold today. - Quantity sold for each product. - Remaining
stock. - Products running low. - Out-of-stock items. - Sales value. -
Pending settlements (optional).

Vendor Portal Each vendor can: - View only their own products. - View
sales. - View remaining inventory. - Download reports. - Receive
notifications. - Update product information (optional).

Store Owner Controls - Approve vendor products. - Change prices. -
Adjust inventory. - Transfer stock. - Suspend vendors. - View all
vendors in one dashboard.
